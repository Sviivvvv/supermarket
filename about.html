ABOUT US.

Welcome from GroceryStore! It gives us great pleasure to introduce ourselves and tell you our story. At GroceryStore, we think that great customer service, high-quality goods, and a love of the community can all enhance people's lives. Established in the center of Colombo in 2024, GroceryStore soon gained popularity as a key component. Our constant commitment to quality and client pleasure has allowed us to develop into a successful supermarket. We take great satisfaction in finding the best items and making sure each one satisfies our exacting requirements. From farm-fresh food to gourmet treats and everyday home necessities, our extensive assortment offers something for everyone's palate. Our experienced and welcoming staff is here to help you at every turn, making your shopping experience genuinely pleasurable. Giving back to our communities via partnerships and committed efforts is something we are passionate about. GroceryStore is here to help, whether you're a family looking for healthy ingredients, a busy professional, or a selective shopper. We are honored that you choose us.
Sincerely, Savindu

Our Available Branches

Our Colombo Branch

Located in the heart of Colombo, our grocery store offers a vibrant shopping experience amidst the bustling city life. Situated conveniently within reach of major landmarks and public transportation hubs, our store provides a diverse range of fresh produce, pantry staples, and specialty items. With a focus on quality and affordability, we cater to the diverse needs of the urban community, ensuring a seamless shopping experience for residents and visitors alike.

Our Nugegoda Branch

Nestled in the suburban charm of Nugegoda, our grocery store provides a tranquil shopping environment away from the city's hustle and bustle. Surrounded by lush greenery and serene residential neighborhoods, our store offers a curated selection of locally sourced fruits, vegetables, and household essentials. With ample parking and a welcoming ambiance, we strive to serve the close-knit community of Nugegoda with personalized service and top-notch products, making grocery shopping a delightful experience for all.
